Murree hills

Murree is a popular hill station and a summer
resort in Pakistan, Murree is specially for the
residents of Islamabad.

Samundar Katha Lake itself is a small, beautiful

Sari Paya

Siri Paye is a lush green meadow and is not easily
accessible. It is located in the Shogran
Valley, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba is home to the largest ski resort in
Pakistan. The area also contains two Buddhist stupas
and six monasteries that are scattered around the resort.
